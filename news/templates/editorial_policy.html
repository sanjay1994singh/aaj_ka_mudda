Editorial Policy

Our Content Standards

We aim to publish responsible, clear and reader-focused news content.

Accuracy

We try to verify important facts before publishing. When official information is available, it is preferred as a source for sensitive or public-interest stories.

Corrections

If a factual mistake is found, we aim to review and correct it as soon as possible. Readers can send correction requests through the Contact Us page.

Separation of News and Promotion

Sponsored or promotional content should be presented clearly and should not mislead readers as independent reporting.

Images and Media

Images are used for news presentation, reporting, illustration or user-submitted content. Credit and correction requests can be sent to the editorial team.

Reporter Submissions

Reporter access is approval-based. Submitted content may be edited, rejected or unpublished if it does not meet editorial or legal standards.
