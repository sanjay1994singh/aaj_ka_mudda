About Us

Aaj Ka Mudda

A Hindi news platform focused on public issues, ground reports, local updates and responsible journalism.

Who We Are

Aaj Ka Mudda is a digital Hindi news portal created to bring important public issues, local news, politics, lifestyle updates, e-paper editions and community stories to readers in a simple and accessible format.

Our goal is to keep readers informed with fast updates, clear presentation and coverage that connects directly with everyday concerns of people.

Our Mission

To publish timely, useful and reader-focused news while giving space to local voices, public concerns, social issues and verified information.

Founder

Aaj Ka Mudda is founded by Manoj Kumar, with a vision to build a public-interest Hindi news platform that highlights real issues, local voices and ground-level updates.

What We Cover

  • National, state and local news
  • Politics, public issues and administration updates
  • Delhi NCR, Uttar Pradesh and regional coverage
  • Lifestyle, articles, videos and YouTube updates
  • Daily e-paper editions and archive-style reading
